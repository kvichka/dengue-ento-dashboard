Epidemic Threshold — 2026 vs Historical Corridor

National monthly cases compared against a historical corridor built from 2018-2025 (8 years): Endemic (blue line) = historical mean for that month; Epidemic (red line) = mean + 2 standard deviations. Green shading = normal range, yellow shading = elevated but below epidemic level. This is the standard "endemic channel" method used in outbreak surveillance, not arbitrary bands. 2026 (dark bars) only plotted through the last reported month.
